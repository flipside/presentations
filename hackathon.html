How not to waste time at a hackathon

  1. judging - know the criteria
  2. presentation - you're judged by what you say/show
  3. demo - only show the interesting parts
  4. code - only build what you show

Idea Tips

  1. solve a painful problem
  2. check for originality
  3. simple is good
  4. add a twist to make it intersting
limit to 1 new technology per person on team

Judging Tips

  1. judges, sponsors and partners can have different criteria
  2. it's also important to understand the judging format
  3. if you don't know your audience criteria, ask!
  4. the best hacks exceed expectations
also startup viability

Presentation Tips

  1. time is limited
  2. don't waste time on boring stuff
  3. state the problem clearly
  4. demo the solution
  5. highlight potential
  6. timed practice
  7. seriously, PRACTICE!

Building Tips

  1. build a minimally viable hack
  2. focus on what people can see
  3. it only needs to work once
  4. limit to one new technology at a time
  5. optional stretch goals
  6. fake it till you make it
(nobody expects you to build a complete, functional product)

General Tips

  1. get lots of feedback
  2. ask for help when you need it
  3. consider being strategic about prizes
  4. don't be shy, make new friends
  5. build something cool and have fun!
